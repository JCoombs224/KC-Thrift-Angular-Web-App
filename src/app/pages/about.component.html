About KC Thrift Store

Web Drops

Web drops at KC Thrift Store occur every other week. This allows us to refresh our inventory and continue to give our customers new and exciting items that they can enjoy near and far.

Shop Policies

  • All online sales are final (No returns/exchanges)
  • Shipping for a fee is available
  • We also offer free in store item pick up days every Monday/Friday
  • Store Credit is not accepted for online purchases
  • Item holds are not accepted

Store Hours

  • Open everyday from 10am - 7pm
